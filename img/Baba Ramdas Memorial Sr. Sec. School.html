Baba Ramdas Memorial Sr. Sec. School

  • Our Mission
  • Why BRDM School..??

We at BRDM School pledge to lead the children to find and develop their 'God Given Talent' and their learning potential to the fullest to become a self reliant citizen, a fine human being, socially aware, humanistic, compassionate and kind, having pride in his/her country and believe in peace, harmony and brotherhood, which is reached in an invigorating and competitive atmosphere, created by excellent facilities, exposure and guidance provided by energetic, enthusiastic, talented and dedicated faculty.

The educators at our school sincerely believe that the school is a happening venue that provides the impressionable children with wonderful learning experiences that assist them in their pursuit for excellence. The efforts are geared to help students become promising individuals who possess a good moral character & cultivate in them the desirable virtues of discipline, devotion , mercy and empathy. The school has a blend of modern outlook and the rich Indian culture. It aims at developing personalities, instilling in children the qualities of leadership, cooperation & collaboration.

TOUR AND TRIPS




Children are virtuous manifestations of God. When exposed to various novelties and oddities of life, they react in different ways, which reflects their instinctive understanding of the phenomena around them. This is here when the mature ones ought to realise their onus to identify their inbuilt abilities and interests so as to provide the nascent with befitting situations that assist them in realising the same for their holistic growth. If their untapped potential is channelled in a constructive manner, they will certainly grow as cognizant, skilled and emotionally sound individuals. It is indeed an onerous task and we at BRDMschool are sincerely devoted to uphold the legacies established by the divinely inspired educators. We have designed our curriculum in such a way that the latent talents of our children are tapped to the fullest and they become efficient, responsible and ideal citizens of tomorrow.

Sh. JAGDISH KAUSHIK

Principle

Knowledge does not comprise all that is contained in a large term of education. The feelings are to be disciplined, the passions are to be restrained, true and worthy motives are to be inspired and pure morality is to be inculcated under all circumstances. The learning process has never been so dynamic as it is today. The perception of education is constantly undergoing a tremendous change. It would give education a global dimension and to make teaching and learning a never ending quest for excellence, we need to keep pace with the changing times. Using the right technology & tools, teachers can now mould students into efficient & proactive citizens of the world.

Sh. NARENDER BHARDWAJ
