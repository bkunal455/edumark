History & Genesis

Baba Ramdas Memorial society was founded in the year 1994 with eminent people from various walks of life as its members. It started its pioneer work in the name of Baba Ramdas Memorial School affiliated to Haryana Board in the center of Kharkhoda with the humble beginning of 90 students. Their excellent academic record elevated the school within a short period, to be one of the best schools in Kharkhoda. The first April of 1994 observed a new dawn when Sh. JEETRAM KAUSHIK, a great visionary & educationist , planted a sapling named BRDM SCHOOL at Pai (Sonepat), Haryana with a hope that it would become a shady boon for younger generations to acquire knowledge, experience, skills and right attitudes for a better conduct of life. With the passage of time, the dream turned into reality as the school has now got the denomination of being a premier institution of India that attracts students from different states of the country and from abroad as well. Its entire ambience and infrastructure have been inducted to meet the scholastic challenges of 21st century. BRDM thrives on the dictum that each day is an opportunity plore and enunciate new ways of imparting quality education that suits the temperament of modern day learners. Considering the needs of changing times, it has got tremendous links with leading schools abroad, which assists it in unifying the aims of education globally, especially in the times of cultural and ethnic upheavals and when a peaceful existence seems to be so elusive. The proud achievements of its alumni are persistently adding to its perennial glory and ethics it stands for. Not basking in the glory of their achievements, they desired to open the door of whole new world to the children of Pai, Kiroli, Sohati who are unaware of the modern trend of education and opportunities. This dream took shape in the form of Baba Ramdas Memorial School, Pai (Sonipat), with a very beautiful, well equipped and exquisitely planned building in a serene environment, never seen before at Kharkhoda. It is a perfect place to develop the heart, mind and soul of a child.

HBSE Boarding School

BRDM School is one of the most prominent boarding schools at Pai affiliated to the Board of School Education, Haryana, India. It conforms to the curriculum designed by the Board in letter & in spirit. It adheres to all the rules & regulations laid by the HBSE and ensures that the students willingly participate in different scholastic and co-scholastic activities that render them a productive and joyous stay on the Kharkhoda campus. Over the years it has attained the denomination of being a locale that grooms prodigies in the multifarious domains of schooling. An on Kharkhoda campus boarding facility is pre-requisite for better education is our guiding motto. The entire fabric of boarding system has been wreathed in a way that the trained mature ones exercise good influences upon the nascent for their all-round growth. Even the preparation of meals undergoes meticulous observations and quality check. The administrative staff, visiting parents and students dining together stands testimony to transparency in systems at BRDM School . While striving to maintain the requisite competitive edge in studies and sports, children at times find it difficult to deal with stress. For this, there are periodical counselling sessions that also embody the essence of moral values depicted in various religions and faiths.

Why BRDM Schools?

The educators at our school sincerely believe that the school is a happening venue that provides the impressionable children with wonderful learning experiences that assist them in their pursuit for excellence. The efforts are geared to help students become promising individuals who possess a good moral character & cultivate in them the desirable virtues of discipline, devotion , mercy and empathy. The school has a blend of modern outlook and the rich Indian culture. It aims at developing personalities, instilling in children the qualities of leadership, cooperation & collaboration. There is a mindful blend of curricular and co-curricular activities that helps unveil the latent talents of students. It takes pride in its state-of- the- art edifices that prompt pupils to explore the world by themselves.

Vision & Mission

We at BRDM School pledge to lead the children to find and develop their 'God Given Talent' and their learning potential to the fullest to become a self reliant citizen, a fine human being, socially aware, humanistic, compassionate and kind, having pride in his/her country and believe in peace, harmony and brotherhood, which is reached in an invigorating and competitive atmosphere, created by excellent facilities, exposure and guidance provided by energetic, enthusiastic, talented and dedicated faculty. BRDM School does understand its onus to transform the nascent into the enlightened who can be of an efficient service to the nation and the community at large. In the present times of regional and communal upheavals, it is essential that children at an impressionable age strive to attain academic excellence and at the same time, learn to transcend all unnatural barriers for a peaceful existence. The school takes due care while weaving the fabric of scholastic & co-scholastic activities so as to create an ambience wherein pupils not only appreciate such subtleties but also practise them in real life situations.
